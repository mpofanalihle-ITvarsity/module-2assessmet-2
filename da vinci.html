DA VINCI'S

Situated on trendy Kloof Street, Da Vincis is famous for thin based pizzas, delicious burgers, huge hearty salads, spare ribs, wings, calamari and fish and chips. Our staff greet regulars by name and make sure everyone feels at home in our relaxed and friendly environment. We offer in seat dining, as well as take aways through us directly, UberEats, MrD and Bolt.
